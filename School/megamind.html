Megamind

Megamind is a movie about a villain turned good once his enemy was killed. The movie follows the perspective
of the ‘Super Villain’ Megamind, the movie starts off with him falling to his death, then rewinds
to the very beginning. Megamind was raised as an outcast, which caused him to blossom into a villain,
he was always outdone by his nemesis ‘Metro Man’. Even as a child ‘Metro Man’ would outdo him,
which helped Megamind become a villain and Metro Man a hero. After Metro Man’s fall, Megamind grew bored
of having control of Metro City and decided to create a new hero to battle, but the new hero, ‘Titan’,
grows mad with power, and becomes a villain too, this causes Megamind to convert to a hero to save Metro City.
Megamind’s change is abrupt but noticeable, it is also influenced by Roxanne Ritchi, a news reporter.
Megamind impersonates Metro Man to scare away Hal/Titan, but his plan backfires when his mispronounces
Metro City as ‘metrosity’, which Hal/Titan knows Megamind does frequently. Megamind’s change of character
is a classic switch from good to evil, which is present in most movies that battle with the idea.
